About Me

Though Design is my forte, I have an ecclectic set of skills which I have put to use in the various projects I have completed and worked on over the course of the past half decade When it comes to design my focus is to make each project a unique experience that can speak to those who play or otherwise interact with it. To achieve this, I always start with a theme, then wherever possible make every facet of the game work towards reinforcing that theme.

Game Designer - Team Hydra
Septemer 2023 - present

Team Hydra is new studio that is made up of me and a handful of classmates from my university course. Initially made for the sake of doing group work, it has since metamorphosed into something we are proud enough to want to develop post-university. This is because over the past 2 years, we have been working on our own stealth game (a demo of which is on my itch.io page) and we believe that is has the right stuff for Tranzfuser. This being a game development competition with winners receiving funding for their game.

For the team, I have worked as one of 2 game designers. Working closely with the other we came up with the game itself, and as of late I have been in charge of all things related to the level design.

A Treatise Concerning Correct Combat Conduct for Officers in the Theatre of War
This project is an interactive art piece, as well as a game rolled into one. I constructed it by hand, designed the pieces and the board myself, as well as learned photography just to document it for others to see. It is a commentary on the romanticism surrounding glory in battle, especially further back in time, as this game is set in the Napoleonic era.
